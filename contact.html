FAQ

Are doctoral positions paid?
All doctoral positions are fully funded, including social benefits.
For doctoral programs, do I need to know German?
No, knowledge of German is not required.
Do I need to have a MS degree to apply for the doctoral program?
No, a MS degree is not required.
For short-term internships, will MPI help with immigration?
Yes, MPI will help provide documentation required for doing short-term internships in Germany.
